About Us

Since 2011, the company "SUPER TRADE CLIP" LLC has been supplying large enterprises producing meat products with meat, minced meat and skin products from broiler chickens. This year, in order to increase the volume of production and expand the range of products, a production line of high-quality chicken cutlets, nuggets and dumplings was launched. The products manufactured at our enterprise are produced on the basis of European technology, relying on our national values.
